Smoked Ribs

Ingredients

  • Pork Baby Back Ribs
  • mustard, or binder of your choosing
  • barbque rub of your choosing
  • apple cider vinegar
  • butter

Cooking Directions

  1. Prep your meat by peeling off the membrane from the Back side, easly peeled by picking up the edge with a knife and using a paper towel to gain grip
  2. Lather mustard or binder of your choice in a thin layer over the entire rack of Ribs
  3. Administer a generous coat of the seasonings of your choice
  4. Place ribs in the smoker uncovered at 250 degrees F. spraying with the vinegar every 30 minutes for 2 hours or until it builds a nice dark hard crust
  5. After 2 yours remove your ribs place butter on top and wrap in butcher paper and continue to smoke at 275 degrees for another 2 hours
  6. After 2 hours uncover place back in the smoker at 275 uncovered for 1 hour or until falling apart. Spray with apple cider vinegar every 30 min
  7. Once pulling apart by its own weight remove cut into individual Ribs and serve
