Tiramisu

  1. 2 med eggs
  2. 1 egg yoke
  3. 130g caster sugar
  4. 500g mascarpone
  5. 300g sponge finger biscuits
  6. 300-400g strong brewed coffee, chilled
  7. 20g cocoa powder, unsweetened

Cooking Directions

  1. Combine cofee with caster sugar in a bowl and set to the side
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the coffee and caster sugar mix til incorporated
  3. Dip sponge fingers into the coffee lightly and place in a 9x9 baking pan, the bottom should be lined with the sponge fingers.
  4. Scoop half of your mascarpone onto the top of the lady fingers, repeat this process for a second layer
  5. Sift cocoa powder on top of the second layer of mascarpone cream
  6. Cover and place in the fridge fo at least 6 hours before serving
